Sponsors & Supporters

In addition to the generous provision of accommodation from CodeBase, Prewired is grateful for the support it has received, both as in-kind donations and as funding, from Edinburgh-based tech companies, most notably Port80 and Skyscanner and Kotikan. Please contact us if you are interested in becoming a sponsor.
